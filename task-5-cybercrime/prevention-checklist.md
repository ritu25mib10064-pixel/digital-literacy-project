Title: Stay Safe from UPI Fraud

Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials
Do not scan unknown QR codes to receive money (receiving money never requires PIN)
Always verify the identity of the sender before clicking on any link
Avoid downloading apps from unknown or untrusted sources
Use only official and verified banking or UPI applications
Enable screen lock, fingerprint, or face lock on your phone
Regularly check your bank transaction messages and alerts
Do not respond to calls or messages claiming fake rewards, cashback, or lottery winnings
Never approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps
Keep your mobile number and banking apps secure and private
Update your apps regularly to avoid security vulnerabilities
Immediately block your account if you notice any suspicious activity
Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in
Call Helpline Number: 1930 for urgent help
