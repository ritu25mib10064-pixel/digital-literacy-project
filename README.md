## Project Overview
This project is based on Digital Literacy, where I have completed different tasks to understand how to use digital tools effectively and responsibly. The aim of this project is to develop awareness about safe internet usage, professional communication, and online platforms.

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and useful tools for students. This helped me understand how to present information visually.

## Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I learned how to build a professional online presence which will help me in future internships and career opportunities.

## Task 3 – Platforms Exploration
I explored coding platforms and completed a beginner-level challenge. I also created a Google Form for a digital literacy quiz. This task helped me understand collaboration tools and online learning platforms.

## Task 4 – Email Etiquette
In this task, I wrote two professional emails and created a social media checklist. I learned how to communicate properly in a professional environment and the importance of responsible social media usage.

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I created a case study on UPI payment fraud and a prevention checklist. This helped me understand different types of cybercrimes and how to stay safe online.
